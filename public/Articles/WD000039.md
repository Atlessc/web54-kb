# WD000039: Printers for PC
Updated by **Tyler Smith on 01/16/2024**

1. Open file explorer
2. ENTER `\\PRDSDCAPP10746\NAM_Secure` into the search
2. Press **OPEN** & **Start Printing**
3. Make sure to select **NAM_Secure** when you are printing and **NOT "follow you"**

## First Time Printing on HP Printers:**
1. Scan Badge
2. Login Manually with the same credentials you would use to log into your computer
3. Press Print All
4. When finished, ***DON'T FORGET TO LOG OUT.***

***After your first print, you will only need to scan badge and
not have to log in manually.***
